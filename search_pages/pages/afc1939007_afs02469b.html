  • These fiddle tunes are being recorded by Joe Cloud in Odana, Wisconsin, on October
  • the 16th, 1938, for the Archive of American Folk Song in the Library of Congress.
  • Mr. Cloud is 53 and has the blood of the Chippewa Indians flowing in his veins.
  • He has played the fiddle since he was 15 years old and learned to play from his father, who
  • was also a fiddler.
  • He plays entirely by ear.
  • The notes born on the violin tuner are G on D, E and A.
